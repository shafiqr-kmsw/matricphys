Key Insights:

• Equipartition Theorem: Each degree of freedom contributes (1/2)kT to the average kinetic energy

• Monoatomic particles can only translate in 3D space

• Diatomic particles can also rotate about two perpendicular axes

• At the same temperature, diatomic particles have higher total kinetic energy due to rotational motion

• The simulation shows both translational and rotational motion for diatomic particles
